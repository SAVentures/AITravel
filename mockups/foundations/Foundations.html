Concentric is the craft move. Inner radius = outer − padding (16 − 12 = 4), so corners nest instead of pinching.

06
What we refuse
the anti-slop pledge

The three choices this system makes on purpose — a tinted neutral field, an expressive face, a single accent — sit one step from current AI tells. They are earned here, never reflexive. These are out, full stop:

✕
Gradient fills & gradient text. The #1 AI tell — and exactly what the old version did. Solid semantic tones only.
✕
The reflex faces. Inter, Geist, Space Grotesk, Instrument Serif, Fraunces. They signal “no decision.”
✕
Borders & hairline strokes. No separators, no outlines — separation is white-on-grey surfaces, soft shadows, and space.
✕
Glass as decoration. Only the system material, only on floating chrome, only over a real layer.
✕
Cards inside cards and the icon-tile-over-heading template. Group with space, not nesting.
✕
Bounce / elastic motion and gratuitous tap-scale. One damped curve; one continuous motion.
✕
Em-dash & buzzword copy. Specific, plain, verb-led — no “streamline / empower,” no exclamation marks.
✕
Off-grid spacing (10/13/14/18/22) and tight line-height. Everything on the 4/8 ladder.
